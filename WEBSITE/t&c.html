Parkease Parking Service Terms and Conditions

User Types

There are two types of users:

  1. Wallet Users: Registered users who have a digital wallet associated with their account. Parking fees are automatically deducted from their wallet balance.
  2. Guest Users: Users who do not register themselves. They can pay using the dynamic QR code at the exit points.

Wallet Creation

To create a wallet, users must provide the following information:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Password
  • Registered Vehicle Number

Wallet and Payments

  1. Upon successful registration, wallet users will have a digital wallet associated with their account. Users can add funds to their wallet using the payment methods available on the Parkease platform.
  2. By adding funds to your wallet, you authorize Parkease to charge the selected payment method for the amount specified.
  3. In the event of any technical issues or failures while adding money to the wallet, users may request a refund. Refunds for failed wallet transactions will be processed within 1-2 business days.
  4. Wallet funds are non-transferable and non-refundable except as required by law. Once parking fees are deducted from the user's wallet upon entry and/or exit from Parkease parking facilities, no refund will be provided.
  5. Users are responsible for ensuring that their wallet has a sufficient balance to cover parking fees. Failure to maintain a sufficient balance may result in additional charges or penalties.
  6. The minimum amount to be added to the wallet during top-up is Rs. 10.00.
  7. Wallet users will receive a flat 2% discount on their parking bill.

Parking Usage and Fees

  1. Wallet users may utilize Parkease parking facilities located in participating public spaces, such as malls and commercial complexes, where Parkease parking is available. Parking fees will be automatically deducted from their wallet balance upon entry and/or exit.
  2. Guest users are users who do not register themselves. They can pay using the dynamic QR code at the exit points. They are responsible for paying the parking fee before exiting the parking facility.
  3. Parking fees may vary depending on the location and specific parking facility.
  4. Users must ensure that a minimum balance of Rs. 5.00 is available in their wallet before entering a Parkease parking facility. Failure to maintain the minimum balance may result in denial of entry.
  5. If the parking bill amount exceeds the available wallet balance, wallet users are requested to top up their wallet immediately and try again with the billing process.
  6. Please note that due to system processing, in rare scenarios, there might be a delay of up to 1-2 hours for the updated wallet balance to reflect after adding funds.
