Parkease Parking Service Refund Policy

At Parkease, we strive to provide a satisfactory experience for all our users. However, we understand that there may be circumstances where you need to request a refund. This Refund Policy outlines our guidelines for refund requests.

Refund Eligibility

We may issue refunds in the following situations:

  • Technical errors or failures resulting in duplicate charges
  • Errors in billing or overcharging
  • Unsatisfactory service experience due to Parkease's fault

Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact our customer support team at parkeasetesting@gmail.com. Please provide the following information:

  • Your name
  • Email address associated with your Parkease account
  • Description of the issue and reason for the refund request
  • Any relevant documentation or evidence supporting your request

We will review your request and may require additional information before processing the refund.

Refund Timeframe

Refunds for eligible requests will be processed within 1-2 business days.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Refund Policy, please contact us at parkeasetesting@gmail.com.
